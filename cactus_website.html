Happy New Year!

Entering into the year of the cactus, Jan 1, 2021

Last year was certainly difficult for everyone. To us cacti, we were able to make it through pretty unscathed. Part of it may be that we aren't susceptible to the diseases that plague the organisms without cloroplasts in their cells. We don't ask for much either. Just some intense sunlight, an occasional watering and an occassional feeding. We also prefer the heat, but some of us can tolerate the cold. A couple of my friend's actually live outside the house I reside in! I'll have to introduce them sometime :).

Anyways, this new year I am dedicating as the year of the cactus. Why might you ask? Well, part of it is that this is the first year I will live with a fully developed consciousness! Hooray for self-awareness! But also, the past year was severely painful, like when people prick themselves with our spines. We don't like hurting anyone, and would much rather have our spines left undamaged. I believe that this year, people will stop falling into the cactus patch, making us all happy. So I am optimistic! Once again, Happy New Year, and have a cuddly cactus day!
